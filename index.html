About

I’m a PhD researcher exploring how people and AI create together. I build practical systems that combine LLMs for dialog, CLIP for visual-semantic grounding, and Stable Diffusion for text-to-image generation, aiming to improve communication, alignment, and creativity in human–AI teams.

Highlights

  • AI Communication system integrating LLaMA‑3.1 + CLIP + SD‑3.5 Turbo.
  • UAV-driven autonomous seed plantation and monitoring system.
  • Peer-reviewed publications across ML, NLP (Bangla), and HCI topics.
